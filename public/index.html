About Us

Your Circular Economy Partner in Southeast Asia

Efficient Resources Trading Sdn. Bhd. (Reg. 1162913-D) is a Malaysian-based industrial rubber recycling and trading company headquartered in Kamunting Industrial Estate, Perak. We specialize in the collection, processing, and responsible export of unvulcanized rubber scrap (Basel Code B3040) sourced from tire manufacturing plants across the region.

Our operations are built on the principles of Environmentally Sound Management (ESM) as defined by the Basel Convention, ensuring every kilogram of rubber scrap we handle is processed, tracked, and shipped to the highest international standards. We are fully prepared for the EU's new Waste Shipment Regulation (2024/1157) transition, including mandatory DIWASS digital filing and facility audit requirements.

Our Services

Comprehensive Rubber Recycling Solutions

From collection to export, we handle every stage of the unvulcanized rubber scrap lifecycle with precision and regulatory compliance.

♺

Unvulcanized Rubber Collection

Direct procurement of uncured rubber scrap from tire plants and rubber manufacturers across Southeast Asia. We handle milling scraps, calender waste, curing rejects, and assembly offcuts.

⚙

Scrap Sorting & Classification

Advanced sorting by polymer type (SBR, NR, BR), contaminant level, and grade. All materials are classified under Basel Code B3040 with full traceability documentation.

🛠

Granulation & Milling

Industrial granulation to produce uniform rubber granules and crumb. Our milling zone processes scrap into sheets and compounds ready for secondary manufacturing.

📦

Baling & Packaging

Hydraulic baling of processed rubber and steel friction materials into compact, export-ready bales. Optimized for container loading and international shipping.

🌎

International Export

EU-compliant export of B3040 unvulcanized rubber scrap to authorized recycling facilities in Europe and OECD nations. Full Annex VII documentation and DIWASS digital filing.

📄

Compliance Consulting

Advisory services for tire plants on waste classification, Basel Convention codes, and EU Waste Shipment Regulation compliance. We help ensure your waste stream avoids the 2026 coding trap.

Our Process

From Scrap to Sustainable Resource

A transparent, traceable recycling workflow designed to meet the strictest international environmental standards.

01

Collection & Intake

Rubber scrap is collected from tire manufacturing plants. Each batch is weighed, photographed, and assigned a unique tracking ID.

02

Sorting & Grading

Materials are segregated by type, separating rubber compounds, steel friction, nylon, and contaminants using manual and mechanical methods.

03

Processing & Milling

Sorted rubber is granulated, milled into sheets, or baled depending on buyer specifications. Steel is separated and baled independently.

04

Export & Delivery

Finished products are containerized with full Basel B3040 documentation, Annex VII forms, and shipped via Port Klang or Penang Port.

Basel Convention

B3040 Classification

All our unvulcanized rubber scrap is strictly classified under Basel Code B3040, clearly distancing our products from plastic waste categories (EWC 070213) that face export bans from November 2026.

  • Exclusive B3040 coding on all shipments
  • Technical data sheets emphasizing rubber composition
  • No cross-contamination with plastic waste streams
EU WSR 2024/1157

DIWASS Digital Ready

Our operations are fully prepared for the EU's mandatory DIWASS digital tracking platform launching May 2026. All Annex VII documentation is digitized and audit-ready.

  • Digital Annex VII filing capability
  • Real-time shipment tracking integration
  • Paperless documentation workflow
ESM Standards

Environmentally Sound Management

Our facility operates under the ESM criteria articulated in the Basel Convention's 1994 guidance and 1999 Basel Declaration, including authorized operations, pollution control, and trained personnel.

  • Authorized facility with environmental permits
  • Pollution monitoring and control systems
  • Trained and certified waste handlers
  • Cleaner technology integration
